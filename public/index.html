Training Tips

  • Kit check: trail shoes with grip, headlamp for dusk runs, lightweight shell.
  • Technique: hike the steeps, open your stride on descents, relax shoulders.
  • Weekly plan: Tue intervals, Thu steady trail, Sun long run.
  • Safety: whistle & phone mandatory for night sessions.
